CoDev 4E Limited is a forward thinking and an innovative Company, whose core area of operation include land access, resettlement, livelihoods, grievance management, community development and stakeholder engagement. The firm is dedicated to helping purpose driven Companies plan and implement resettlement and community related development programmes geared towards creating positive change and yielding a win-win situation for project proponents and affected communities.

The firm comprises of a team of experts with over 25 years in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience in planning and implementing resettlement, livelihoods, community engagement and community development programmes.

Core Values

CoDev 4E is guided by the following core values that guide our internal and external relations;

  • Effectiveness
  • Efficiency
  • Empowerment
  • Enhancement

Our Mission

To organize resettlement and related activities on a sustainable basis to the satisfaction of the communities and project proponent.

Our Vision

To ensure a win-win situation for project proponents and host communities.

Our Aims and Aspirations

  • To effectively provide top-notch resettlement and community development and any other related services acceptable to our clients
  • To empower communities to realize their dreams and aspirations
  • To create enabling environment for communities to freely and fully participate in development programmes
  • To efficiently use the resources available to achieve the desired results with integrity
  • To improve upon what we do step-by-step
