WHO WE ARE

RCS Consult is a fully owned Ghanaian Company duly registered by the Registrar Generals Department with a Certificate to commence business issued by the Registrar of companies.

RCS Consult is a Resettlement, Community Development, Grievance Resolution and Compensation Management Company in Ghana that specialises in land access facilitation and management for mining companies.

The Company provides professional services such as;
  • Resettlement planning and implementation and its related issues, grievance resolution, document controls and data management, socio-economic survey, livelihood Restoration programs, land access planning and management; farm parcel and land ownership survey prior to land acquisition;
  • Post moratorium compensation management, compensation negotiations planning and management;
  • Compensation assessment in accordance with the Minerals and Mining Act and LI 2175;
  • Land monitoring prior to commencement of operations,
Land management during various phases of mine project development and advisory services on all other land access and compensation issues.
